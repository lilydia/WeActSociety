Lydia Li
Founder & Event Coordinator

Lydia is the event coordinator and founder at WE ACT. She drives the organization's vision, strategy and growth as it provides interesting and unique ways for people to save energy and changes the lives of its community. Under Lydia's leadership, WE ACT stands at the forefront of raising the awareness of water conservation, and has educated over 1500 people in the community and spread its branches to 3 local schools.
